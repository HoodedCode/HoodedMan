The "Hooded Man" game does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertisements, or data sharing. Your privacy is important to us, and we ensure that no external services are involved in your gaming experience.

Security

Since the game operates offline and does not collect or store personal data, security concerns related to data storage and transmission do not apply.
